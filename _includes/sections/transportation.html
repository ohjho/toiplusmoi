Traveling to Lawrence

Lawrence is located just south of Interstate 70 in Eastern Kansas.

If you're driving from the north via I-35 South, take exit 8B in Kansas City to I-29 North, then exit 3B to I-635 South, then exit 4B to I-70 West to Lawrence. Take exit 202 from I-70 and head south on Iowa Street. Centennial Park will be on your right after you exit the tunnel under 6th Street.

If you're planning to fly in, the closest airport is Kansas City International. The airport is about a one hour drive from the venue and you will likely need to rent a car to get to Lawrence (as there is unfortunately no regular shuttle or bus service).
