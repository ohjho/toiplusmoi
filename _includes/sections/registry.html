Registry

Matt and Sarah have been living together for two years, and have acquired most essential household goods in that time, so they were somewhat reluctant to register for gifts… until they discovered Zola registries. The online registry is non-conventional: it includes options like contributing to a life fund, or gifting an experience.

For guests who prefer to give a material gift, there are some included on the registry website — but please know that any heirloom, hand-made, or otherwise unique gifts are absolutely appreciated!

If you will be giving a registry item acquired through a different platform, you may send an email to bridesmaid Mary Claire Chao and request that a gift be marked as 'purchased' on Zola.
