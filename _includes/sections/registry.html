Registry

Véro and John have been living together for awhile, and have acquired most essential household goods in that time, so they are reluctant to register for gifts.

They take delights in the simplest of pleasures and are always inspired by the minimalist lifestyle. Like most millennials, they too highly value experiences over possessions. So the time that we'll have together on 31 March 2018, will already be itself a valuable gift.

If you do feel so compelled, we hosted a registry on Thankful. The Thankful online registry is non-conventional: it includes options like contributing to a life fund, or gifting an experience.

For guests who prefer to give a material gift, there are some included on the registry website — but please know that any heirloom, hand-made, or otherwise unique gifts will also be highly valued!

If you will be giving a registry item acquired through a different platform, you may send an email to the groom and request that a gift be marked as 'purchased' on Thankful.
