Come party with us in Lawrence!

Sarah Chao and Matt Menzenski are tying the knot, and you're invited!

The wedding will take place in Lawrence, Kansas at Centennial Park's Eastern pavilion (see the map below) on Saturday, June 3, 2017. The pavilion is a covered, outdoor space with banquet-style picnic tables and a small deck area. The park itself is a sprawling green space with a playground, restrooms, skate park, and disc-golf course.

The ceremony is scheduled for 5:00pm, rain or shine. Drinks, local barbecue, and dessert to follow. Guests are welcome to arrive anytime after 4:00pm. We will be outdoors, enjoying the sunny weather (historically, temperatures average 65°F on June 3 in Lawrence), playing lawn games, and eating barbecue. Feel free to wear whatever you will be comfortable in.

In the unlikely case of extreme or inclement weather, please check this webpage for any last-minute adjustments to the wedding schedule.
