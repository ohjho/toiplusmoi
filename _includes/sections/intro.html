Come party with us in Tai Hang!

The wedding will take place in Tai Hang, Hong Kong at Tipsy Bar & Restaurant (see the map below) on Saturday, March 31, 2018. The restaurant features an open bar and dining area with interior matching the colonial era style of the hotel.

The neigbhourhood, Tai Hang, itself is a trendy area where local shops and quirky cafés sit side by side.
