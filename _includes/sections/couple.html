Sarah and Matt

Sarah Chao, a native of southern Minnesota, and Matt Menzenski, a native of central New York, met in Lawrence, Kansas, in August 2014 in the University of Kansas's Department of Slavic Languages & Literatures, where Matt was entering his second year in the graduate program and Sarah was beginning her first.

It didn't take long for Sarah and Matt to realize that they had a lot in common. They quickly bonded over a love of cheesy nineties songs, brunch, Russian, and dumb jokes (okay, that last one is really just Matt). Soon they were spending every minute together.

In early 2015, Matt and Sarah moved in together, and Dave the Cat joined the family.

Sarah and Matt both left KU in 2016 and now work in Kansas City. Sarah is an older adult care manager for Jewish Family Services, where she helps Russian-speaking clients access resources and navigate the aging journey. Matt is a software engineer at PayIt, a Kansas City startup focused on improving citizen-government interaction.

Matt and Sarah both love their jobs and are looking forward to starting the next chapter of their lives together in Kansas City.
